About Four wheeler Insurance

Why Four wheeler insurance is necessary ?

Risk Covers like car insurance is to protect us from unforeseen events and unplanned liabilities. Anyone can get stuck on the road with their car after an accident, car engine failure due to rains or theft of the vehicle. In any of the cases, having a car insurance policy will be a savior.


Cover against natural and man-made disasters.

In the event of a natural disaster in your area, the financial plan of your Four-wheeler insurance policy will cover your Car against damages caused by floods, lightning, cyclones, storms and other natural phenomena. Similarly, the Car insurance plan also protects your Four-wheeler against damages caused by man-made acts of violence such as terrorism and vandalism.


Personal Accident Cover.

While Driving your car, if an unfortunate road accident leaves you with major or minor injuries, your Four wheeler insurance can cover all kinds of personal injuries you may have suffered. While the injury itself can be a cause of mental grief, the financial protection offered by the personal accident cover can considerably mitigate the inconvenience!

Standalone own damage Car Insurance Policy.

Our Standalone Own Damage Car Insurance Policy is beneficial in cases where an unfortunate accident can leave your Car damaged. While the Own Damage cover cannot handle third-party liabilities, your Car can receive financial protection against damages to the Car caused by natural and man-made disasters and fire. This wheeler policy also offers reimbursement in case of theft of the Car.

Protection from legal fines.

It is illegal to Drive your Car without a valid third-party Four-wheeler insurance policy. You could attract hefty legal and traffic fines for not carrying a bike insurance policy along. Even within the city limits, it is advisable to always keep your Four wheeler insurance policy with you to avoid the hassle of getting into trouble with the law.
